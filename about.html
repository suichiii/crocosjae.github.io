WHO

Greetings! I'm Jae, a passionate crochet artist, where a loop and twist of yarn ignites boundless creativity. Whether diving into traditional patterns or venturing off the beaten path, I revel in the journey of weaving tales with each stitch, crafting pieces that echo my distinct style and spirit.

WHAT

The crocosjae website serves as a hub for showcasing a variety of crochet products while offering a platform for commissioning diff. client's custom pieces. Visitors can explore an assortment of meticulously crafted items and easily connect with the artist to bring their crochet visions to life.

WHY

Crocheting is my hobby, where I find comfort and fulfillment in crafting unique pieces with yarn and hook. Each creation holds a piece of my heart, a testament to my love for the craft and the joy it brings me.
